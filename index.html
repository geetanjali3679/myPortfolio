Local F&B · Hyderabad

From invisible to fully booked —
a restaurant's local SEO turnaround

A family-run restaurant wasn't showing up on Google Maps at all — not even for its own name.

+340% Organic traffic
#1 Google Maps rank
3× ROAS Google Ads
The problem

No local visibility, no footfall

The restaurant had zero Google Business Profile optimisation, duplicate listings confusing the algorithm, and no location-specific keywords on their site. Competitors with worse food were ranking on page 1. Every new customer was coming from word-of-mouth only.

What I did
  • Audited and merged duplicate GBP listings; rewrote every field with geo-targeted copy
  • Built 6 hyper-local landing pages targeting "restaurant near [neighbourhood]" keywords
  • Launched a review-generation strategy that took them from 18 to 94 Google reviews in 10 weeks
  • Set up a tightly geofenced Google Ads campaign targeting a 5km radius with lunch and dinner dayparting
  • Fixed Core Web Vitals (LCP was 8s → brought to 2.1s) to stop losing mobile visitors
The outcome

Ranked #1 on Maps, tables filled on weekends

Within 90 days the restaurant ranked #1 on Google Maps for their primary category in their area. Organic website traffic grew 340%. The Google Ads campaign delivered a 3× return on ad spend, with a cost-per-reservation 60% lower than the industry benchmark. The owner reported being fully booked on Friday and Saturday evenings for the first time.

E-commerce · United States

Cutting ad spend in half while
doubling e-commerce revenue

A US product brand was burning $12k/month on Meta Ads with a 1.1× ROAS — essentially paying to break even.

−52% Cost per sale
4.2× ROAS (was 1.1×)
+210% Organic traffic
The problem

High spend, near-zero profit from ads

The ad account had 47 active ad sets with no clear winner — budget was spread too thin to exit the learning phase. Landing pages were generic and slow (4.8s load). There was no retargeting, no email capture, and no SEO — 100% of revenue depended on paid traffic. One algorithm change could have wiped them out.

What I did
  • Consolidated 47 ad sets to 8 tightly-themed campaigns; killed ₹1.4L/month in wasted spend
  • Built a full-funnel Meta structure: TOF (broad interest), MOF (video view retargeting), BOF (cart abandon + purchaser lookalikes)
  • Rewrote ad copy to lead with the problem, not the product — CTR jumped from 0.8% to 3.1%
  • Redesigned landing pages with benefit-first hierarchy and social proof above the fold
  • Launched SEO targeting mid-funnel buying-intent keywords to reduce dependency on paid
  • Set up GSC + GA4 attribution to track real revenue, not just clicks
The outcome

Profitable ads + organic traffic as a safety net

ROAS jumped from 1.1× to 4.2× in 60 days on a reduced budget. Cost per sale dropped 52%. The SEO work kicked in at month 4, bringing 210% more organic traffic and reducing ad dependency from 100% to 61% of revenue. The brand now has a sustainable, diversified growth engine instead of an expensive single point of failure.

B2B Services · India

Keyword strategy that turned a
services site into a lead machine

A B2B consulting firm had a beautiful website, zero organic traffic, and no idea why no clients were finding them on Google.

0 → 12 Pg.1 keywords
+180% Inbound leads
−42% Cost per lead
The problem

Ranking for keywords nobody searches

The site was optimised for internal jargon — terms only the company itself used. No potential client would search for them. There was no technical SEO foundation (missing schema, poor crawlability, no internal linking), the blog had 3 posts from 2021, and Google Ads was targeting broad-match keywords that were burning budget on irrelevant queries.

What I did
  • Conducted a full keyword gap analysis against 5 competitors; identified 38 high-intent, low-competition keywords the site had zero presence for
  • Restructured site architecture and rewrote all service pages around buyer-intent search terms
  • Published 8 targeted blog posts answering the exact questions prospects type into Google before hiring a consultant
  • Fixed 14 technical SEO issues: missing H1s, broken canonical tags, no schema markup
  • Rebuilt Google Ads with exact-match and phrase-match only; wrote negative keyword list of 200+ irrelevant terms
  • Optimised for GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) — content now cited in Perplexity answers for 3 target queries
The outcome

Page 1 rankings and a pipeline they can predict

The firm went from 0 to 12 page-1 Google rankings in 5 months. Organic inbound enquiries increased 180% year-on-year. The rebuilt Google Ads account cut cost-per-lead by 42% while increasing qualified lead volume. The client now has a documented, repeatable keyword strategy and a content calendar that keeps compounding results without ongoing ad spend.

Health & Wellness · India + US

SEO + Google Ads integration that
compounded growth across both markets

A wellness brand selling in both India and the US was running two disconnected strategies — each undermining the other.

3.6× Avg. ROAS
+260% Organic sessions
−38% Blended CPA
The problem

Two markets, two strategies, zero synergy

The India and US campaigns had completely different messaging, landing pages, and keyword strategies with no shared learning. They were bidding on the same keywords organically and paid — paying for clicks they should have been earning for free. Budget was split equally between markets despite the US having 4× higher conversion value. Attribution was broken: revenue was being double-counted.

What I did
  • Mapped full keyword intent across both markets; identified where organic ranking made ads unnecessary and vice versa
  • Reallocated 65% of ad budget to the US market where revenue-per-click was highest
  • Built market-specific landing pages (INR pricing, local social proof for India; USD pricing, US testimonials for the US)
  • Created a unified content strategy where blog posts pre-sold the product before prospects reached the ad
  • Fixed GA4 attribution with proper UTM governance and cross-domain tracking
  • Launched Performance Max campaigns in the US with custom audience signals from organic traffic behaviour
The outcome

Integrated growth engine across both markets

Blended CPA dropped 38% in the first quarter. Organic sessions grew 260% over 6 months. The integrated SEO + Ads approach meant organic content warmed up audiences before they saw ads — improving ROAS to 3.6× average across both markets. The brand now has a single strategic roadmap for both markets with clear roles for organic and paid in every stage of the funnel.
